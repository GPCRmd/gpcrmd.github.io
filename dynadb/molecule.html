Simulation Submission
Step 2
Small Molecule Information

FILL IN THE SMALL MOLECULE FORM

Please, provide information of all non-protein molecules in your system.

Small molecule information contains data for compounds and molecules that are not proteins, including solvent and ions. Compound level information can be retrieved from PubChem Compound and CHEMBL databases. User must provide also molecule information data regarding elemental composition, connectivity, net charge, protonation states and tautomerical form through uploading a MDL Molfile or an SDFile. Isotopical composition is optional. PubChem CIDs for typical molecules as water or most common ions will be provided.

Only molecule chemical structures that have coordinates coming from experimental data or that are crucial for the experimental setting of the simulation must be included in model objects (e. g. crystalized-like waters or very stable ions, but not water molecules or ions with randomly generated coordinates). Otherwise chemical structures must be included into a dynamics object.

For more information on this form, see the docs.
