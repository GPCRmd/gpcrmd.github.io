Simulation Submission
Step 1:
Protein Information

FILL IN THE PROTEIN FORM

Fill in the form with the relevant data concerning the receptor involved in the simulation.

Proteins information is linked to a canonical sequence obtained from UNIPROTK SWISSPROT and identified by a UNIPROTK accession number (AC).

Mutated proteins are accepted by specifying wild type UNIPROTKAC and mutated sequence or punctual mutations. Gaps created by insertions and deletions are specified by a hyphen character (‘-’).

Several proteins can be added in a same submission but at least one must be a G-protein coupled receptor (GPCR) whose sequence must be deposit on GPCRdb.

For more information on this form, see the docs.
