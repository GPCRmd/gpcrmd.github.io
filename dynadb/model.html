FILL IN THE MODEL FORM

Fill in the form with information on the curated pdb protein (loops, mutations, etc)

Contains structural information that describe proteins or “molecular complexes” in PDB format which contain structure information in a similar way than in PDB database: protein structure models, ligand models, molecules crystallized with proteins, crystallized or predicted stable waters or ions, but not solvent, ions, membrane lipids or other molecules which position is randomized or not specified when building the system (i.e. structure without the atoms added during neutralization, solvation and ionitzation of the system).

Protonation state residue name aliases (e.g. ASPP, GLUP, HSP, HIP) are allowed and structures must contain explicit hydrogen atoms.

For more information on this form, see the docs.
